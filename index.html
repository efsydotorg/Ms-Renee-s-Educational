Welcome to EFSY.org

Faith-rooted instruction in STEM and music for youth and families

Who We Are

EFSY.org is a faith-rooted instructional ministry operating under 508(c)(1)(A) nonprofit status. We produce educational content specifically for our participants and deliver mastery-based instruction in STEM and music.

What We Do

We teach physics, math, music theory, and engineering logic using whiteboards, manipulatives, and digital tools. Our instruction is delivered through spontaneous field engagement, formal library sessions, and remote online teaching.

When & Where

Instruction takes place year-round in New York City libraries (Brooklyn Public Library and New York Public Library systems), public spaces, and online platforms. We are listed in tutoring directories and offer both scheduled and spontaneous sessions.
